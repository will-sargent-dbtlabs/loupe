Loupe Aurora light
print-friendly

The print-friendly Loupe surface: clean light paper carrying the same brand gradient in headings, rules, and marks. What you see on screen is what prints — no glow to drop, no dark ink to invert.

Reads at a glance

Dark ink on near‑white for long-form legibility; the accent gradient does the signaling. Inline code, quotes, and tables sit inside the palette cleanly.

On intent

Design is the rendering of intent — the choices you make until the page stops fighting you.

Notes

  • Default is light — ideal for print and client PDFs.
  • Switch to the Aurora content-theme for the dark brand version.
  • Accent gradient = the brand blue→purple→orange.

About this theme

The light half of the aurora pair. Same grid, type, and gradient accents as the dark theme, on paper — so screen and print match.
